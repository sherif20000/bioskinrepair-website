How to Tell If Your Skin Barrier Is Damaged (And What to Do Next)

Your skin barrier — the outermost layer of the epidermis — is your body's first line of defense against environmental stressors, pathogens, and moisture loss. When it breaks down, the signs are often misread as "sensitivity" or "dry skin," masking the true underlying dysfunction.

Understanding the specific markers of barrier compromise — from elevated TEWL (trans-epidermal water loss) to altered skin pH — is the critical first step before any repair protocol can be effective.
