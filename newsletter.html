Is this guide actually free? What's the catch?

Yes, it's completely free. There is no upsell, no paid upgrade, and no "premium version." When you sign up, you also subscribe to our weekly skin science newsletter — but you can unsubscribe from that at any time and keep the guide. The only thing we ask is that you read it.

Is the guide appropriate for all skin types and conditions?

The protocol is designed for anyone with a compromised or damaged skin barrier — including those with eczema, rosacea, post-exfoliation sensitivity, and general reactive or sensitized skin. It is not a medical treatment and does not replace professional dermatological advice. If you have a diagnosed skin condition under active clinical management, consult your dermatologist before following any new protocol.

How long until I see results following the 7-day protocol?

The skin barrier renews itself in approximately 28 days, but measurable improvements in hydration, TEWL reduction, and sensitivity can appear within 7–14 days of starting a correct repair protocol. The guide sets realistic expectations: 7 days is the beginning of recovery, not the end. Most readers report noticeably calmer, more comfortable skin by day 5–7 and continued improvement over the following weeks.
