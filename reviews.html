How We Score Products

Every product is evaluated against four formulation criteria. We do not accept payment for placement or allow brands to influence scores.

Criterion 01

Ingredient Quality

We assess ceramide subtypes, active concentrations, and whether clinical-grade ingredients are present at effective amounts.

Criterion 02

Barrier Science Alignment

Does the formula address the actual mechanisms of barrier dysfunction — TEWL reduction, lipid matrix replenishment, microbiome support?

Criterion 03

Formulation Safety

Fragrance status, preservative system, pH compatibility, and suitability for compromised or reactive skin.

Criterion 04

Value for Efficacy

Cost-per-use relative to ingredient quality. A $15 product with a better ceramide profile beats a $90 product built on marketing.
